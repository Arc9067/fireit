fire token

Fire is a token built on Binance smart chaim network with a vision to provide a place for safe investment. It is lead by a well respected team in crypto space. The Fire signifies the zeal and enthusiasm to achieve something greater than the current state. In the same fashion, we aspire to build a community which can proudly back a project having utilities and can generate a steady stream of revenue for itself.

fire token Utility

We plan to build a set of bots/telegram tools which could be used by other projects or investors. These bots would help the project teams to plan their strategies for the growth of their project. Investors would also be able to utilise their bots in order to plan their investment decisions. We have finalised the first bot that we would be building. This bot would be tracking all the buys and sells by investors in any project. Depending on the buy and sell value of each and every investor, the bot would display the details of individual wallet's profits and losses. The purpose of this is to detect the general mood and investment behaviour of investors in any particular project.
